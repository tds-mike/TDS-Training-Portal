New Technician Training Pathway

This roadmap outlines the path from new hire to a certified TDS professional. Each module combines digital learning with essential hands-on practice. A mentor must sign off on each module before proceeding to the next.

Week 1: Foundations - Safety & Shop Operations

Digital Learning

  • Read and understand the entire Safety Guide.
  • Read and understand the entire Chemicals Guide.
  • Locate the SDS binder and understand how to look up a chemical.

Practical Application

  • Locate all emergency equipment (Fire Extinguishers, First-Aid, Eyewash).
  • Demonstrate proper dilution of 3 common chemicals (e.g., Reset, MultiX, PERL).
  • Shadow a senior technician during two full Decontamination Washes.
  • Perform one supervised Decontamination Wash.

Week 2: Interior Restoration

Digital Learning

  • Read and understand the entire Interior Guide, focusing on material identification and tool tiers.

Practical Application

  • Perform a supervised "Basic Interior" service.
  • Practice with the steamer and hot water extractor on test materials.
  • Demonstrate correct leather cleaning technique on a test panel.
  • Shadow a full interior restoration, including coating application.

Mentor Sign-Off: _________________________

I certify that the trainee has demonstrated understanding and competency in all Week 2 modules.

Week 3: Exterior Mastery - Paint Correction

Digital Learning

  • Read and understand the entire Exterior Guide, focusing on PDG use and the levels of paint correction.

Practical Application

  • Demonstrate proper PDG usage and interpret readings on 3 different vehicles.
  • Practice machine control (spreading, cross-hatch, wiping) on a test hood with a finishing polish.
  • Perform a supervised Level 1 Gloss Enhancement on a client vehicle.
  • Shadow a full two-step paint correction and ceramic coating application.

Mentor Sign-Off: _________________________

I certify that the trainee has demonstrated understanding and competency in all Week 3 modules.

Weeks 4-5: Film Specialization (Tint & PPF)

Digital Learning

  • Read and understand the Tint Guide and PPF Guide.

Practical Application

  • Practice shrinking tint film on multiple curved test windows.
  • Perform a supervised tint installation on "2 Fronts."
  • Practice PPF squeegee techniques and edge setting on a test fender.
  • Assist a senior technician with a full front end PPF installation.

Mentor Sign-Off: _________________________

I certify that the trainee is ready for supervised client work in film services.
