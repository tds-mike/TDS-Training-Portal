The TDS Technician Training Curriculum

This is the official curriculum for all new hires. It outlines the structured path to becoming a TDS Certified Core Detailer. Each week builds upon the last, combining digital learning with mentored, hands-on application. Successful completion of the 5-week program and final certification includes a professional tool kit and certification bonus.

Part 1: Core Detailing Certification (5 Weeks)

Week 1: Immersion & Observation

Monday - Digital Immersion:
  • Scenario of the Week: Mentor presents a scenario (e.g., "A client's car has bird droppings etched into the paint. What is the immediate safety concern according to the chemical guide?").
  • Read & Review: Safety Guide & Technician SOP.
  • Read & Review: Chemicals Guide.
Tuesday - Continued Immersion:
  • Read & Review: Exterior Guide.
  • Read & Review: Interior Guide.
Wednesday - Shadow Exterior Workflow:
  • Mid-Week Knowledge Check: Mentor asks 5 questions about safety and chemical pH.
  • Shadow Mentor: Observe a full Decontamination Wash, understanding the "why" behind each step.
  • Shadow Mentor: Observe vehicle inspection, prep, and taping for paint correction.
Thursday - Shadow Interior Workflow:
  • Shadow Mentor: Observe a Full Interior Restoration, focusing on tool use and workflow.
  • Shadow Mentor: Observe shop closing procedures as per the SOP.
Friday - Knowledge Review:
  • Mentor-led Q&A session covering all digital modules. Trainee must demonstrate a strong theoretical understanding of all core services and safety protocols.

Mentor Sign-Off: _________________________

Week 2: Exterior Detailing Mastery

Monday - Learn & Prep:
  • Scenario of the Week: "The water pressure in the wash bay suddenly drops. What is your troubleshooting process?"
  • Re-read Exterior Guide (Decon Wash) & Chemicals Guide.
  • Demonstrate proper dilution of Lift, Reset, IronX, and Ech2o.
Tuesday - Shadow & Assist:
  • Review the Urable inspection report for the client vehicle before shadowing.
  • Shadow a client Decontamination Wash, assisting with wheel cleaning and final wipe-down.
Wednesday - Demonstrate:
  • Mid-Week Knowledge Check: Mentor asks questions about the steps of a Decon wash.
  • Demonstrate correct two-bucket method and Wash Order (WO).
  • Perform a supervised Decontamination Wash on a shop vehicle.
Thursday - Demonstrate:
  • Review Urable report, then perform a Decon Wash on a client vehicle with minimal supervision.
Friday - Certify:
  • **Certification Task:** Independently perform a full Decontamination Wash to TDS Standard (Target: under 60 minutes).

Mentor Sign-Off (Exterior Decon Certified): _________________________

Week 3: Interior Detailing & Restoration

Monday - Learn & Prep:
  • Scenario of the Week: "You find a delicate, 'sticky' soft-touch dashboard. How do you adjust your cleaning process?"
  • Re-read Interior Guide. Focus on material ID and chemical safety.
  • Demonstrate proper setup and cleaning of steamer and extractor.
Tuesday - Shadow & Assist:
  • Review Urable report, then shadow a Full Interior Restoration, assisting with the "Air Purge" and vacuuming.
Wednesday - Demonstrate:
  • Mid-Week Knowledge Check: Mentor asks to identify 3 different interior materials and the correct chemical for each.
  • Demonstrate safe steamer technique and correct leather cleaning.
  • Perform a supervised Basic Interior Detail.
Thursday - Demonstrate:
  • Review Urable report, then perform a supervised Full Interior Restoration on a moderately soiled client vehicle.
Friday - Certify:
  • **Certification Task:** Independently perform a Full Interior Restoration to TDS Standard (Target: under 2.5 hours).

Mentor Sign-Off (Interior Certified): _________________________

Week 4: Paint Correction Mastery

Monday - Learn:
  • Scenario of the Week: "Your PDG shows 85µm on a door but 150µm everywhere else. What does this mean and how does it change your plan?"
  • Re-read Exterior Guide (Paint Correction). Memorize pad/product combos.
  • Demonstrate correct PDG usage and defect identification with an inspection light.
Tuesday - Shadow & Practice:
  • Shadow a full Two-Step (Level 3) Correction.
  • Practice DA polisher control on a test hood for 1-2 hours.
Wednesday - Demonstrate (Level 1 & 2):
  • Mid-Week Knowledge Check: Mentor asks for the correct pad/product combo for a Level 1 and Level 2 correction.
  • Perform a supervised Level 1 Paint Enhancement.
  • Perform a supervised One-Step (Level 2) Correction on a test panel.
Thursday - Demonstrate (Level 3) & Document:
  • Assist mentor on a Two-Step (Level 3) Correction, performing the compounding stage on 2-3 panels.
  • Professional Documentation: Document findings on a test panel in Urable, recommending a Two-Step correction with photos and notes.
Friday - Certify:
  • **Certification Task:** Independently perform a One-Step (Level 2) Correction on a client vehicle to the TDS Standard.

Mentor Sign-Off (Paint Correction Certified): _________________________

Week 5: Ceramic Coating & Final Certification

Monday - Learn:
  • Scenario of the Week: "You notice a coating high spot that's already cured. What are your immediate next steps?"
  • Re-read Exterior Guide (Coatings). Focus on prep, flash times, and high spots.
Tuesday - Shadow & Practice:
  • Shadow a full CQUARTZ UK 3.0 coating application.
  • Practice coating application and leveling on multiple test panels.
Wednesday - Demonstrate:
  • Mid-Week Knowledge Check: Mentor asks to explain the process for fixing a high spot.
  • Demonstrate proper Eraser wipe-down technique.
  • Perform a supervised coating application on a set of wheels.
Thursday - Final Project:
  • **Capstone Project:** Trainee will perform a full "prep and coat" service on a client vehicle (Decon, Level 1 Enhancement, CQUARTZ Lite application) under final review by Shop Manager.
Friday - Certify:
  • Pass the Technician Knowledge Quiz with a score of 90% or higher.

Shop Manager Sign-Off (TDS CORE DETAILER CERTIFICATION): _________________________
