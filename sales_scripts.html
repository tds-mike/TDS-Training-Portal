Sales Scripts & Dialogues

These scripts are your guide, not a rigid set of lines to memorize. Use them to structure your conversations, ensuring you follow the NEPQ framework to uncover needs, build value, and close sales effectively. The goal is a natural, consultative conversation.

Incoming Call: Window Tint

Initial Connection

"Thank you for calling TDS, we specialize in ceramic tint, paint protection film, and ceramic coatings. This is [Your Name], how can I help you today?"

"Great! I'm happy to provide you with a quote for window tinting. To get started, can I get your name and the year, make, and model of your vehicle?"

Situation & Problem Awareness

"Okay, and have you had window tint before, or are you familiar with the different types of film available?"

  • If NO: "Let me quickly explain the options. We primarily use two types of premium film: ceramic and carbon... [Explain benefits of each]."
  • If YES: "Great! Just to make sure we're on the same page, we offer premium ceramic and carbon films..."

"Which of these options are you most interested in?"

Recommendation & Close

"Okay, for [Windows] on your [Vehicle] with our [Film Type] film, the price is [Price]. This includes a lifetime warranty against fading."

"Our installations typically take around 4 hours. We operate by appointment, so let's find a time that works best for you. What day were you thinking?"

Incoming Call: Paint Protection Film (PPF)

Initial Connection

"Thank you for calling TDS... This is [Your Name], how can I help you today?"

"I can certainly help with information on paint protection film. First, what prompted you to consider PPF for your vehicle?"

Situation & Problem Awareness

"Can you describe the current condition of your vehicle's paint? Are there any specific areas you're concerned about protecting?"

"What are your primary concerns about potential damage to your vehicle's paint? Rock chips, scratches...?"

Recommendation & Close

"Based on what you've described, our Full Front End package would be the ideal solution to protect against those rock chips. For your [Vehicle], that would be [Price]."

"If our paint protection film service aligns with your needs, are you ready to take the next step and schedule an appointment?"

Incoming Call: Ceramic Coating

Initial Connection

"Thank you for calling TDS... This is [Your Name], how can I help you today?"

"Excellent, I can help with that. What about ceramic coatings interested you enough to call us today?"

Situation & Problem Awareness

"Tell me more about your vehicle. What challenges have you faced in maintaining its exterior in the past?"

"How frequently do you find yourself washing and waxing your vehicle to keep it looking its best?"

Recommendation & Close

"It sounds like maintaining your car's exterior can be time-consuming. Based on our discussion, our [Package Name] ceramic coating would be a great solution. For your [Vehicle], the price is [Price]."

"Are you ready to take the next step toward protecting your vehicle and making it easier to maintain?"
