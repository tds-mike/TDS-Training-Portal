Sales Process Checklist

Customer: ____________________
Vehicle: ____________________
Date: ____________________

Phase 1: First Impression

Follow up with new lead (Call > Text).
Create and send a formal Proposal in Urable.
Send "What to Expect" email to build value.

Phase 2: Onboarding & Authorization

Convert Proposal to Job/Invoice in Urable WITH customer.
Enter VIN, Plate, and Mileage accurately.
Perform full digital inspection with photos in Urable.
Obtain customer signature on digital inspection.
Conduct consultative walk-around, noting customer concerns.
Use displays and paint gauge to educate and plant upsell seeds.
Present the physical TDS Welcome Packet (Brochures, etc.).
Verbally review all Approval Documents and set final expectations.
Obtain digital signatures for ALL required approval documents.
Secure 50% deposit for the job.
Mark vehicle as "Dropped Off" in Urable.

Phase 3: Communication During Service

Send 50/50 or before/after photo as first progress update.
Present any technician-recommended upsells with photo evidence.
Send "nearing completion" text to build anticipation.

Phase 4: Handoff & Long-Term Loyalty

Showcase the transformation with before/after photos on a tablet.
Educate on aftercare and provide all physical warranty booklets.
Recommend and sell appropriate maintenance products.
Proactively schedule their first maintenance appointment.
Obtain "Work Completed" signature in Urable.
Take final payment and record transaction.
Launch referral program with 3 physical cards.
Set expectation for automated review request.
(3-7 Days Later) Make personal follow-up call/text.
