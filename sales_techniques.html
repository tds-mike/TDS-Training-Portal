Mastering the Sales Process SOP

This is the central roadmap for the entire customer journey at The Detail Shop. It outlines the process from the first inquiry to post-service follow-up, and shows how each of our sales modules fits into this workflow.

Phase 1: Lead Intake & Follow-Up

The goal is to provide a rapid, professional response to every inquiry and convert them into a scheduled consultation. All leads from all sources are to be managed within the GoHighLevel platform.

Inquiry Handling Protocol

A structured and persistent follow-up process is crucial for maximizing lead conversion.

  • Initial Response Time:** Inquiries received before 2:00 PM must be followed up on the same day. Inquiries after 2:00 PM are followed up the next morning.
  • Daily Follow-Up (First 5 Days): Follow up daily. The required process is to **always call first, then immediately send a text.** If the call is not answered, leave a voicemail.
  • Weekly Follow-Up (Next 4 Weeks):** Follow up once per week. For this phase, **call only** and leave a voicemail.
  • Monthly Follow-Up (Ongoing):** Follow up once per month indefinitely. You can use a call or text for this long-term follow-up.

Use the Follow-Up Log

Every call and its outcome (e.g., customer objections) must be logged. This data is vital for refining our Sales Scripts and Objection Handling guides.

Phase 2: The Onboarding & Consultation Process

This is the most critical phase where we build trust and value. This process begins the moment the customer arrives for their drop-off.

Vehicle Drop-Off & Inspection Checklist

  • Create Invoice & Enter Data: While with the customer, create a new invoice in Urable. Enter all customer and vehicle information (VIN, plate, mileage).
  • Perform Vehicle Walk-Around:
    • Take photos of any pre-existing damage and upload them to the "Inspection" section in Urable.
    • Physically point out damages to the customer and ask if they have any areas of special concern. This is where you use your NEPQ questioning skills.
    • **Plant Upsell Seeds:** Use your product knowledge of Tint, PPF, and Coatings to identify and suggest opportunities based on the vehicle's condition.
  • Set Final Expectations: Clearly review the scope of work, the expected outcome, and the estimated completion time. Answer any questions.
  • Secure Approval & Deposit: Obtain a customer signature for the work approval in Urable. Take a 50% deposit or full payment.
  • Introduce Referral Program: Briefly discuss the referral program incentives while processing the payment.

Phase 3: During the Service

Maintain communication and transparency while the vehicle is in our care.

Progress Updates & Quality Control

  • Technicians will upload 50/50 and before/after photos to Urable.
  • Use these progress photos to update the customer, especially if it presents an opportunity for an upsell or requires approval for additional work.
  • Ensure the job is on track for the promised pick-up time.
  • Once work is complete, the vehicle must be quality checked to ensure it meets the expectations set during onboarding.

Phase 4: Vehicle Pick-Up & Post-Service Loyalty

The final step is to deliver a professional handoff that ensures customer satisfaction and encourages future business.

Vehicle Handoff & Closing Checklist

  • Walk the customer around the vehicle, showcasing the completed work.
  • Obtain a signature on the "Work Completed" approval form.
  • **Educate on Proper Maintenance:** Explain aftercare and recommend specific products.
  • **Schedule Next Appointment:** Proactively offer to schedule their next maintenance wash or service.
  • **Take Final Payment:** Process the remaining balance.
  • **Reinforce Referral Program:** Hand the customer 3 referral cards and encourage them to put their name on them. Remind them of the benefits.
  • **Ask for a Review:** Inform the customer they will receive an automated link from Urable in 24 hours asking for a review and that we would appreciate their feedback.

The Referral Program Explained

When a new client uses a referral code, they get **10% off** their service. The referrer receives a credit of **$25 to $100** in their account based on the new client's invoice total, which can be used for future services or products.
