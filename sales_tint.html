Selling Window Tint

1.1 What is Window Tint?

Window tint is a multi-layered polyester film applied to the interior of a vehicle's windows. Its primary purpose is to reduce the amount of visible light, heat, and UV radiation that passes through the glass.

Core Benefits for the Customer:

  • Glare Reduction: Improves driving safety and comfort by reducing glare from the sun and headlights.
  • Heat Rejection: Keeps the cabin significantly cooler, reducing the reliance on air conditioning.
  • UV Protection: Blocks over 99% of harmful UV rays, protecting occupants and preventing interior fading and cracking.
  • Privacy & Security: Enhances privacy and conceals valuables from view.
  • Aesthetic Enhancement: Gives any vehicle a sleek, premium appearance.

1.3 Connecticut Tint Law & Medical Exemptions

It is our responsibility to inform customers of the legal regulations to ensure compliance.

  • Windshield: Non-reflective tint allowed only above the manufacturer's AS-1 line.
  • Front Side Windows (All Vehicles): Must allow more than 35% of light in (35% VLT).
  • Rear Windows (Sedans): Must also be 35% VLT or lighter.
  • Rear Windows (SUVs/Vans/Trucks): Any darkness can be used.
  • Medical Exemptions: A valid medical exemption allows for tint as dark as 20% VLT.
