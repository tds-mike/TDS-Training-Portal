Advanced Pathway: Window Tinting Certification

This 3-week curriculum is for Certified Core Detailers specializing in window tinting. The goal is to build the technical skill, precision, and efficiency required to become a professional TDS film installer.

Week 1: Fundamentals & Film Manipulation

Monday - Learn:
  • Re-read the Tint Guide entirely.
  • Practice plotter usage: Load film, select patterns in CORE, and weed 5+ jobs.
Tuesday - Practice Shrinking:
  • Spend 3-4 hours on a test rear windshield practicing the traditional "H-pattern" shrink until consistent.
  • Spend 2 hours on a test side window mastering the "Snap-Shrink" method.
Wednesday - Practice Hand-Cutting:
  • Shadow mentor on a full tint job, focusing on their prep and installation workflow.
  • Spend 3 hours on test glass practicing hand-cutting patterns for side windows.
Thursday - Demonstrate Prep:
  • Perform a supervised, surgical-level glass prep on a client vehicle's side windows.
  • Assist mentor on a "2 Fronts" installation, handling the prep and liner removal.
Friday - Certify Fundamentals:
  • **Certification Task:** Flawlessly shrink a rear windshield and a side window for mentor review.

Mentor Sign-Off: _________________________

Week 2: Side Windows & Installation

Monday - Learn & Shadow:
  • Review installation methods in the Tint Guide (Seal Removal vs. Two-Stage).
  • Shadow a full tint install, focusing on how the mentor avoids contamination.
Tuesday - Demonstrate "2 Fronts":
  • Perform a supervised installation of "2 Fronts" on a client vehicle.
Wednesday - Demonstrate "2 Fronts":
  • Perform a second supervised installation of "2 Fronts", aiming to improve speed and reduce contamination.
Thursday - Full Vehicle Assist:
  • Assist on a full vehicle tint, installing all side windows under supervision.
Friday - Certify Side Windows:
  • **Certification Task:** Independently install "2 Fronts" to TDS Standard (no creases, minimal contamination, clean edges).

Mentor Sign-Off: _________________________

Week 3: Complex Glass & Certification

Monday - Rear Windshields:
  • Assist mentor on two rear windshield installations.
Tuesday - Rear Windshields:
  • Perform a supervised rear windshield installation.
Wednesday - Full Vehicle Supervised:
  • Perform a full tint installation on a client vehicle under mentor supervision.
Thursday - Final Project:
  • **Final Project:** Perform a full tint installation with minimal supervision, reviewed by Shop Manager.
Friday - Certify:
  • **Final Review:** Shop manager inspects final project and discusses readiness for independent work.
