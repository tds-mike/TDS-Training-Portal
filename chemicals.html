Chemicals Guide

The Science of Cleaning

Understanding the fundamental types of chemicals we use is crucial for safety and effectiveness. Each chemical is a specialized tool designed for a specific job. Using the right product ensures efficiency and prevents damage to sensitive surfaces like paint, leather, and plastic.

Key Chemical Components

  • Surfactants: Found in car wash soaps, they reduce surface tension to lift and emulsify dirt and grease, allowing them to be washed away easily.
  • Solvents: Dissolve sticky and oily residues like tar, grease, and adhesives. Isopropyl alcohol and mineral spirits are common examples.
  • Abrasives: Fine particles found in polishes and compounds that physically wear away imperfections to remove scratches and restore shine to paintwork.
  • Protectants: These are waxes, sealants, and coatings that provide a protective barrier against UV rays and contaminants, enhancing shine and durability.

Understanding the pH Scale (0-14)

The pH scale is critical for choosing the right chemical for the task. It measures how acidic or alkaline a substance is. Using the wrong pH can damage surfaces.

Acidic (pH Below 7)

Effective at removing mineral deposits, water spots, and rust. Examples include CARPRO Descale and Spotless 2.0.

Neutral (pH 7)

Gentle and safe for regular maintenance washes. It cleans effectively without stripping existing waxes, sealants, or coatings. Our primary neutral soap is CARPRO Reset.

Alkaline/Basic (pH Above 7)

Excellent at cutting through grease, oils, and heavy organic grime. Used for pre-washing and degreasing. An example is CARPRO Lift.

Comprehensive Chemical Arsenal & Dilution Ratios

Exterior Wash & Decontamination

Lift: Alkaline pre-wash foam. High pH to dissolve heavy road film. Dilute 1:10.
Descale: Acidic soap. Removes stubborn mineral deposits and water spots. Dilute 1:10.
Reset: pH-Neutral maintenance soap. Dilute 1:1500 (1oz per 4 gallons).
Hydro2 Foam: Wash and seal soap. Leaves SiO2 protection. Dilute 1:10.
IronX: pH-Neutral iron remover. Use RTU.
Tar X: Solvent-based tar & adhesive remover. Use RTU.
TriX: Iron & Tar remover combo. Use RTU.
BugOut: Intensive bug remover. Use RTU.
Spotless 2.0: Strong acid water spot remover. Use with caution. Use RTU.
Ech2o Concentrate: Dilutions: 1:200 (Rinseless Wash), 1:40 (Clay Lube), 1:25 (Quick Detailer), 1:15 (Waterless Pre-Soak).

Exterior Protection & Finishing

Eraser: Oil & polish remover. Essential pre-coating wipe. Use RTU.
Reload: SiO2 spray sealant (3-6 month protection). Use RTU.
EliXir: Quick detailer for high gloss. Use RTU.
Release: Wax-based spray for protecting fresh coatings. Use RTU.
Clarify PHOBIC: Glass cleaner & sealant combo. Use RTU.

Interior Cleaning & Protection

MultiX: All-purpose cleaner for heavy interior tasks. Dilute 1:20.
Inside: Leather & vinyl cleaner. Dilute 1:1 or 1:2.
InnerQD: Light cleaning and anti-static protection. Use RTU.
SO2Pure 2.0: Odor eliminator. Use RTU.
Clarify: Ammonia-free glass cleaner. Use RTU.

Wheels, Tires, & Dressing

WheelX: Dedicated wheel cleaner. Use RTU.
ReTyre: Heavy-duty tire & rubber cleaner. Use RTU.
DarkSide: High gloss tire sealant. Use RTU.
PERL: Versatile dressing. Dilutions: 1:1 (Tires), 1:3 (Engine/Exterior Plastic), 1:5 (Interior).

Film Installation Solutions

ImmoGel: PPF slip solution concentrate. Dilute 1:100 with water.
Tint Slip Solution: Baby shampoo or Tint Slime. Mix 1oz per gallon of water.
Tack Solution: For setting PPF edges. Mix 20% Isopropyl Alcohol with 80% water.
