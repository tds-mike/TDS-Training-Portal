Chemicals Guide

Understanding the pH Scale

The pH scale (0-14) is crucial for choosing the right chemical. 7 is neutral (water). Below 7 is acidic (removes minerals). Above 7 is alkaline/basic (removes grease/organics). Using the wrong pH can damage surfaces.

Comprehensive Chemical Arsenal

Exterior Wash & Decontamination

Lift: Alkaline pre-wash foam. High pH to dissolve heavy road film before contact. Dilute 1:10.
Descale: Acidic soap. Removes stubborn mineral deposits and water spots. Strips old waxes/sealants. Use for coating prep. Dilute 1:10.
Reset: pH-Neutral soap. Our primary soap for maintenance washes. Safely cleans without stripping protection. Dilute 1:1500.
Hydro2 Foam: Wash and seal soap. Cleans and leaves behind a layer of SiO2 protection. Dilute 1:10.
IronX: pH-Neutral iron remover. Dissolves embedded ferrous particles (industrial fallout) from paint/wheels. Turns purple on contact. Use RTU.
Tar X: Solvent-based tar & adhesive remover. Quickly dissolves tar, sap, and adhesive residues. Apply with a microfiber. Use RTU.
TriX: Iron & Tar remover combo. A time-saver for heavily contaminated vehicles. Use RTU.
BugOut: Intensive bug remover. Enzymatic formula breaks down bug remains to prevent paint etching. Use RTU.
Spotless 2.0: Water spot remover. Strong acid blend to dissolve heavy mineral deposits. Use with extreme caution. Use RTU.
Ech2o Concentrate: Highly versatile. Dilutions: 1:200 (Rinseless Wash), 1:40 (Clay Lube), 1:25 (Quick Detailer).
Optimum No Rinse: Alternative for rinseless washes. Dilute 1:256.

Paint Correction Compounds & Polishes

UltraCut: Most aggressive compound. For removing deep scratches and severe defects, including sanding marks.
ClearCut: Fast-acting compound. Excellent for heavy defect removal with less dusting.
Fixer: One-step polish/compound. Removes moderate swirls and scratches while leaving a high-gloss finish.
Reflect: Finishing polish. Ultra-fine abrasives create a deep, jeweled, swirl-free finish. The final step before protection.
Essence: All-in-One primer polish. Provides minor correction, extreme gloss, and leaves a layer of SiO2 primer to prepare for CQUARTZ coatings.
3D Speed: All-in-One alternative. A cleaner wax that provides light correction and leaves a layer of protection.

Exterior Protection, Coatings & Finishing

Eraser: Oil & polish remover. The essential final step before applying any coating, sealant, or PPF to ensure a pure, clean surface for bonding. Use RTU.
Reload: SiO2 spray sealant. Our standard for 3-6 month protection. Provides immense gloss and hydrophobicity. Use RTU.
EliXir: Quick detailer. A blend of Reload and Ech2o for insane gloss and slickness. Perfect final wipe-down product. Use RTU.
CQUARTZ Lite: User-friendly ceramic coating. Provides ~1 year of protection.
CQUARTZ UK 3.0: Our primary professional coating. Excellent durability (3+ years) and chemical resistance.
DQUARTZ: Premium, high-density coating. Offers the highest level of protection and durability (10+ years).
CQUARTZ Leather 2.0: Durable coating for leather surfaces. Provides UV protection and prevents dye transfer.
CQUARTZ Fabric 2.0: Super-hydrophobic coating for all fabric surfaces. Repels water and stains.
Clarify PHOBIC: Glass cleaner & sealant. Cleans glass streak-free while applying a hydrophobic coating to repel water. Use RTU.
Release: Wax-based spray. Used for safe delivery of freshly coated vehicles to protect from elements during the initial cure window. Use RTU.

Interior Cleaning & Protection

MultiX: All-purpose cleaner. A powerful degreaser for heavy interior cleaning on plastics, carpets, and fabrics. Dilute 1:20.
Inside: Leather & vinyl cleaner. Foaming action lifts dirt from leather grain and plastics without drying them out. Dilute 1:2.
InnerQD: Interior quick detailer. Cleans light dust and fingerprints while adding anti-static properties and a clean, matte finish. Use RTU.
SO2Pure 2.0: Odor eliminator. Reacts with light to break down organic odors, not just mask them. Use RTU.
Clarify: Glass cleaner. Ammonia-free and safe for all tinted windows. Streak-free results. Use RTU.

Wheels, Tires, & Dressing

WheelX: Dedicated wheel cleaner. Safe for most wheel finishes. Breaks down brake dust and road grime. Use RTU.
ReTyre: Tire & rubber cleaner. High-foaming cleaner that pulls out old dressing and browning from tire pores. Use RTU.
DarkSide: High gloss tire sealant. A durable, non-slinging tire sealant for a deep, rich shine that lasts weeks. Use RTU.
PERL: Plastic, Engine, Rubber, Leather dressing. A water-based, versatile dressing. Dilutions: 1:1 (Tires), 1:3 (Engine/Exterior Plastic), 1:5 (Interior).

Film Installation Solutions

ImmoGel: PPF slip solution concentrate. High viscosity allows easy positioning and squeegeeing. Dilute 1:100 with water.
Tint Slip Solution: Baby shampoo or Tint Slime. Provides lubrication for tint application. Mix 1oz per gallon of water.
Tack Solution: For setting PPF edges. Neutralizes slip solution to promote adhesion. Mix 20% Isopropyl Alcohol with 80% water.
