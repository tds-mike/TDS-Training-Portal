Printable Job Checklists

Use these checklists to ensure every step of our core services is completed to the TDS Standard. Select a checklist below and use your browser's print function.

Decontamination Wash Checklist

Phase 1: Prep & Wheels

Initial vehicle inspection & photos
Wheels, tires, and wells cleaned
Touchless pre-soak with Lift
Thorough rinse of pre-soak

Phase 2: Decontamination

Two-bucket contact wash (WO)
IronX chemical decontamination
TarX chemical decontamination
Clay bar/towel mechanical decontamination

Phase 3: Final Steps

Thorough final rinse
Crevices blown out with air
Towel dry with drying aid
Door jambs wiped clean
Tires dressed
Exterior glass cleaned

Full Interior Detail Checklist

Phase 1: Dry Prep

Personal items removed & bagged
Floor mats removed
Full air purge of all crevices
Meticulous dry vacuum of all surfaces

Phase 2: Deep Cleaning

Headliner spot cleaned
Dash, console, and door panels cleaned
Vents and tight areas steamed/brushed
Leather surfaces cleaned
Carpets & cloth seats shampooed/extracted

Phase 3: Finishing & Protection

Plastic/vinyl surfaces dressed (PERL)
Leather surfaces conditioned/coated
Fabric surfaces coated (if applicable)
Interior glass cleaned
Door jambs wiped clean
Floor mats cleaned & reinstalled
Final vacuum

Final Quality Control Checklist

Exterior Check

No polish/wax residue in panel gaps
No coating high spots visible
Tires dressed evenly, no sling
Exterior glass is streak-free
No water drips from mirrors/emblems

Interior Check

No dressing streaks on plastics/vinyl
Interior glass is streak-free (viewed from outside)
Seat tracks & pedals are clean
All personal items returned to vehicle
Vehicle smells clean and neutral
