The TDS Master Sales Framework

This is the blueprint for our sales success. We move beyond simple price quotes to become trusted consultants. Our process is built on a simple but powerful premise: Diagnose, Educate, Recommend.

1. Diagnose

Understand the client's true needs, problems, and desired outcomes through skilled questioning.

2. Educate

Position our services as the solution, building value and demonstrating our expertise.

3. Recommend

Offer a tailored solution and provide a clear, confident path to securing the sale.
