Advanced Pathway: PPF Certification

This 4-week curriculum is for Certified Core Detailers specializing in Paint Protection Film. PPF requires extreme attention to detail. This program builds the foundational skills and advanced techniques for a flawless, invisible installation.

Week 1: Prep & Squeegee Fundamentals

Monday - Learn:
  • Re-read the PPF Guide, focusing on the "Non-Negotiable Prep Protocol."
  • Demonstrate correct mixing of ImmoGel (slip) and Tack solutions.
Tuesday - Practice Squeegee Technique:
  • Spend 4-5 hours on a test hood practicing the "Float, Anchor, Squeegee" method with scrap film. Goal is a bubble/lift-free finish.
Wednesday - Shadow & Assist:
  • Shadow a Full Front End installation, assisting with surgical prep and final wipe-downs.
Thursday - Demonstrate Small Parts:
  • Perform a supervised installation on headlights and mirrors.
Friday - Certify Fundamentals:
  • **Certification Task:** Independently prep and install film on a test hood to TDS Standard.

Mentor Sign-Off: _________________________

Week 2: Complex Curves & Bumpers

Monday - Learn & Practice:
  • Review advanced sections of the PPF Guide on stretching and heat use.
  • Practice conforming film on a test bumper with a steamer.
Tuesday - Shadow & Assist:
  • Shadow a bumper installation, assisting with cleaning and positioning the film.
Wednesday - Demonstrate Bumper:
  • Perform a supervised bumper installation on a client vehicle.
Thursday - Demonstrate Fenders:
  • Perform a supervised fender installation, focusing on wrapping edges.
Friday - Certify Bumper Install:
  • **Certification Task:** Independently install a bumper kit to TDS Standard.

Mentor Sign-Off: _________________________

Week 3: Full Front End & Edge Work

Monday - Hoods:
  • Perform a supervised full hood installation.
Tuesday - Full Front End Assist:
  • Assist mentor on a Full Front End, installing the hood and fenders under supervision.
Wednesday - Edge Work Mastery:
  • Spend 3-4 hours on test panels practicing advanced edge wrapping and corner techniques.
Thursday - Supervised Full Front End:
  • Perform a full front end installation on a client vehicle under mentor supervision.
Friday - Review & Refine:
  • Mentor reviews Thursday's install and provides detailed feedback for refinement.

Mentor Sign-Off: _________________________

Week 4: Quality Control & Certification

Monday & Tuesday - Final Project:
  • **Final Project:** Perform a complete Full Front End installation with minimal supervision.
Wednesday - Quality Control:
  • Learn the post-install QC check. Inspect your own final project for any flaws (lift lines, bubbles, contamination).
Thursday - Troubleshooting:
  • Mentor creates common issues on a test panel (lift lines, small bubbles). Trainee must identify and fix them.
Friday - Certify:
  • **Final Review:** Shop manager inspects final project and discusses readiness for independent work.
