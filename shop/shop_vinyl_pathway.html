Advanced Pathway: Vinyl Wrapping Certification

This 4-week curriculum is for Certified Core Detailers specializing in Vinyl Wrapping. The program focuses on the meticulous prep, material handling, and advanced techniques required to produce a flawless, paint-like finish.

Week 1: Fundamentals & Flat Panels

Monday - Learn:
  • Re-read the Vinyl Wrapping Guide entirely.
  • Practice with a heat gun to understand the "reading" and softening points of scrap vinyl.
Tuesday - Practice Squeegee Technique:
  • Spend 4-5 hours on a test hood practicing "glassing out" and squeegee technique. Goal is a flawless, bubble-free surface.
Wednesday - Shadow & Assist:
  • Shadow a full color change wrap, assisting with surgical prep and hardware removal.
Thursday - Demonstrate Fenders:
  • Perform a supervised installation on two test fenders, focusing on trimming and tucking edges.
Friday - Certify Fundamentals:
  • **Certification Task:** Independently prep and wrap a test hood to TDS Standard, including proper post-heating.

Mentor Sign-Off: _________________________

Week 2: Doors & Inlays

Monday - Learn & Practice:
  • Review advanced sections of the Vinyl Wrapping Guide.
  • Practice using Knifeless Tape to create seams on a test panel.
Tuesday - Shadow & Assist:
  • Shadow a door wrap, assisting with deconstruction (handle/mirror removal).
Wednesday - Demonstrate Doors:
  • Perform a supervised wrap on two client vehicle doors.
Thursday - Inlays:
  • Perform a supervised inlay on a complex recessed area of a test bumper.
Friday - Certify Door Install:
  • **Certification Task:** Independently deconstruct, prep, and wrap a vehicle door to TDS Standard.

Mentor Sign-Off: _________________________

Week 3: Bumpers & Complex Curves

Monday - Mirrors:
  • Perform a supervised wrap on two vehicle mirrors, focusing on triangulation and heat control.
Tuesday - Front Bumper Assist:
  • Assist mentor on a full front bumper wrap, focusing on teamwork to "glass out" the vinyl.
Wednesday - Front Bumper Supervised:
  • Perform a full front bumper wrap on a client vehicle under mentor supervision.
Thursday - Rear Bumper Supervised:
  • Perform a full rear bumper wrap on a client vehicle under mentor supervision.
Friday - Review & Refine:
  • Mentor reviews the week's installs and provides detailed feedback on corner and seam techniques.

Mentor Sign-Off: _________________________

Week 4: Full Vehicle & Certification

Monday & Tuesday - Final Project:
  • **Final Project:** Trainee will be the lead installer on a full color change wrap, with mentor assisting.
Wednesday - Quality Control:
  • Perform a full QC check on the final project. Identify and fix any minor flaws.
Thursday - Troubleshooting:
  • Mentor creates common issues on a test panel (e.g., over-stretching, silvering). Trainee must identify and explain how to fix them.
Friday - Certify:
  • **Final Review:** Shop manager inspects final project and discusses readiness for independent work.

Shop Manager Sign-Off (TDS VINYL SPECIALIST CERTIFIED): _________________________
