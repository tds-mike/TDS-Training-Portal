Technician SOP & Master Workflow

This is the master guide for the entire service process. It integrates our physical work standards with the mandatory Urable workflow. Following this SOP ensures consistency, safety, and the highest quality result for every vehicle.

Phase 1: Job Start & Pre-Work Review

Before you touch a vehicle, you must review the digital work order in Urable. This is a critical step to understand the scope of work and protect both you and the shop.

Pre-Work Checklist

  • Locate the Job in Urable: Find the vehicle's job file on the main Urable dashboard.
  • Review Sales Notes & Customer Concerns: Read all notes from the sales team. Understand their priorities to deliver a better result.
  • Confirm Signed Approvals: **CRITICAL:** Verify that the "Work Authorization" and any service-specific approval documents have a customer signature. **Do not begin work without signed approvals.**
  • Review the Sales Inspection: Look at the photos taken by the sales team. Familiarize yourself with any pre-existing damage.
  • Personal Vehicle Walk-Around: Perform your own inspection. Immediately report any damage not noted in the sales inspection to the Shop Manager.
  • Prepare Your Bay for Efficiency: Gather all necessary tools, chemicals, and pads for the job onto your service cart. An organized bay is an efficient bay.
  • Start the Job Timer in Urable: Once you are ready to begin, find your assigned line item and press the **"Start"** button on the job timer.

Phase 2: In-Progress Documentation & Quality

Your primary focus is executing the service to the TDS Standard. As you work, you must document your progress and findings within Urable to support the sales team and showcase your expertise.

In-Progress Workflow

  • Complete Line Item Checklists: As you finish key steps of a service, check them off in the line item's checklist in Urable. This is our internal QC.
  • Take & Upload Compelling Photos: Your photos are proof of your skill. Upload clear, well-lit 50/50 and Before/After photos to the **"Technician Photos"** section of the Urable job file.

The Technician's Upsell Protocol

When you identify a problem or an opportunity to improve the final result, your expert recommendation is our most powerful sales tool. This is a win for the client (a better result), for sales (an easy add-on), and for you (commission).

  1. Stop & Document: If you discover an issue not covered by the work order, pause your work on that area.
  2. Take Clear Photo/Video Evidence: Capture compelling evidence of the issue (e.g., deep swirls under a light) and upload to "Technician Photos."
  3. Add a Detailed Note: In the main job notes, clearly explain your finding and your professional recommendation.
  4. Notify the Sales Team: Immediately let a salesperson know you have documented an issue in Urable so they can contact the customer for approval.

Example Technician Notes for Upsells:

  • Paint Correction: "During inspection, I found the paint has heavy swirling that the approved Level 1 will not fully remove. To achieve the best gloss, I recommend upgrading to a Level 2 Correction. See attached photos."
  • Wheel Coating: "Wheels are in excellent condition. This is the perfect time to apply CQUARTZ Wheel coating to protect them from brake dust. Recommend adding this service."

Phase 3: Job Completion & Final Finishing

The final 5% of the job is what defines the TDS Experience. This is where we deliver the "wow" factor through meticulous finishing touches.

Universal Finishing Steps (All Jobs)

This is the baseline TDS Signature Finish applied to every vehicle, regardless of the service.

  • Perform a final, meticulous wipe-down of the entire vehicle. No streaks, dust, or residue.
  • Clean all exterior glass and mirrors to a streak-free finish.
  • Wipe down all door jambs and trunk jambs.
  • Apply a branded TDS service sticker to the upper corner of the driver's side door jamb.
  • Place a branded TDS paper floor mat on the driver's side.
  • Hang one TDS air freshener from the rearview mirror.
  • Place a "Thank You" card, personally signed by you, on the passenger seat.

Service-Specific Finishing Touches

In addition to the universal steps, add the following items based on the primary service completed.

  • If Window Tint:
    • Apply "Do Not Roll Down Windows" stickers to window switches.
    • Place a tint aftercare rearview mirror hanger.
  • If Ceramic Coating:
    • Place the official coating manufacturer's warranty booklet and CARPRO aftercare guide in the glove box.
    • Apply the small, corresponding coating sticker to the door jamb.
  • If PPF (Paint Protection Film):
    • Place the official PPF manufacturer's warranty booklet and aftercare guide in the glove box.

Phase 4: Handoff to Sales Team

Your final steps close out your work in Urable and prepare the vehicle for the final quality check by the sales team.

Final Handoff Checklist

  • Perform Final Quality Control: Do one last walk-around of the vehicle in good light. Check for any imperfections.
  • Take & Upload "Completed" Photos: Take high-quality photos of the finished vehicle and upload them to the "Completed Photos" section in Urable.
  • Ensure All Urable Checklists are Complete: Do a final review to ensure every task in your assigned line items has been checked off.
  • Stop the Job Timer: This is your final Urable action. Find your line item and press the **"Stop"** button.
  • Stage the Vehicle: Park the vehicle in the designated delivery area, ready for customer pickup.
  • Communicate Completion: Inform the Shop Manager or Salesperson that the vehicle is 100% complete, has passed QC, and is ready for customer handoff.
